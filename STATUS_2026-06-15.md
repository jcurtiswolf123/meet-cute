## How Jessica Can Get Started

### 1. See the App Live
- Landing: https://meet-cute.fly.dev
- Try signing up with your email at /login
- You'll get a magic-link (check email), click it to enter the app
- Browse a sample member profile (synthetic data for now)

### 2. Access the Operator Studio
- Joshua will create your operator account
- Log in at /studio/copilot (main command center)
- You'll see:
  - **Roster**: all members, searchable, filterable
  - **Pipeline**: breakdown of match stages (suggested → together)
  - **Co-Pilot Chat**: talk to AI about members, suggest matches, book dates
  - **Moderation Queue**: approve/reject photos, resolve reports

### 3. Try the Co-Pilot Commands
In the chat, try:
- "Suggest Alice and Bob for each other" → creates match, notifies both
- "Book the date for Alice and Bob" → opens concierge thread, confirms slot, sends calendar invite
- "Note on Alice: prefers brunch dates" → adds to her profile
- "Who's new on the roster?" → RAG retrieves recent members

### 4. Understand the Member Experience
- Members get ONE suggestion at a time (no infinite feed)
- They see a full profile: photo, name, age, bio, Q&A, mutual friends, vouches
- They click "Yes, introduce us" or "Pass"
- If both say yes, concierge books the table and sends calendar invites
- After the date, optional coaching follow-up

### 5. Review & Approve
- Photos come in as pending
- Go to /studio/moderation to approve/reject
- Reports come in when members flag unsafe behavior
- You resolve them there
